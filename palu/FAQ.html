Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our services.

1. I like this house plan. How can I get it?

Get in touch with us via our Contact Us form, specifying the house plan you want. Kindly provide your phone number, and we’ll revert with all the details. Be sure to check your spam folder for our emails.

2. I like this house plan but I’d like some changes made on it.

We can alter any house plan to suit your needs. Contact us or visit our office to discuss and make the necessary changes. Your satisfaction is our highest priority.

3. When I pay for a house plan, what exactly do I get?

After payment, you’ll receive the main drawings (floor plans, foundation plan, sections, and elevations) and 5 copies of stamped architectural blueprints, sufficient for approval and construction.

4. I want this house plan but before I commit, I would like to meet you.

You’re welcome to visit us at Park Place Business Centre, 2nd Floor, 2nd Parklands Avenue, off Limuru Road. Call us at +254 721318614 to schedule an appointment.

5. Are the house plans pre-approved by the government?

No, our plans are not pre-approved. You will need to seek approval from your county government. We’ll provide all the documents and can facilitate approvals for a nominal fee.

6. Why do I have to pay this much for a house plan?

A good house plan is a critical step in your home investment. Cutting corners can lead to costly modifications later. The cost of a house plan is a small percentage compared to the total cost of construction.

7. How sure am I that this house plan will be approved by the government?

Each county has its own regulations. We’ll assist you throughout the process and make changes if needed to meet approval requirements.

8. I have not found the right house plan that meets my needs.

No problem! We can design a custom house plan based on your specific needs and expectations. Contact us to get started.

9. What about a BOQ?

If you need a Bill of Quantities (BOQ) for financing, we can connect you with a partner Quantity Surveyor to prepare it for you.

10. What about Structural Drawings?

You will need structural drawings from a registered Structural Engineer. These include reinforced concrete and structural timber designs. We can help connect you with an expert if needed.

11. Is there anything else that I require?

We offer additional packages for interior design, electrical and lighting plans, and plumbing services. We can bring in partner engineers for specialized designs or you can work with a skilled fundi.

12. How long will it take for me to get my house plan?

Drawings are ready within 5 working days after payment. Customizations may take longer depending on the changes requested.

13. Are you involved during construction?

We offer construction supervision through site visits. Charges depend on the location, and we may require travel arrangements for areas outside Nairobi.
